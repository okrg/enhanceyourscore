Why Build and Enhance Your Business Credit?

By taking advantage of a properly built and enhanced business credit profile, you will be able to conserve cash flow, obtain better financing terms, limit your personal liability and save money!

 

Protect Personal Credit

By building a creditworthy company, creditors and lenders will be less likely to require a personal guarantee to secure financing.

With corporate credit, your business debts and financial obligations would be reported only on your company’s credit reports—not on your personal reports. As a result, your personal debt to credit limit ratio would not be impacted by the debts of your company.

By separating personal and business credit, you eliminate the risk of commingling funds – and this includes the commingling of credit.

Get Better Credit Terms

Save money by paying lower interest rates and getting better finance terms.

Businesses obtain more favorable rates on lines of credit compared to an individual. For example, you may pay up to 13% interest on a $100,000 line of credit whereas a business could qualify for an interest rate of 7%. That would save you almost $40,000 in interest alone.

Maximize Opportunities

Many lenders, creditors, and suppliers will only extend credit to businesses that meet their corporate compliance guidelines. This includes a business credit listing and ratings with the major agencies.

If you have very little history or no history at all, our credit enhancement program is the fastest and easiest way to build enough credit so that large institutional lenders can approve your applications for business loans, corporate leases, or supplier agreements.
