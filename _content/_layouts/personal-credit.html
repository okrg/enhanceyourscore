Our personal credit enhancement process, sometimes called piggybacking, uses the accounts of another creditworthy borrower to improve your credit rating.
The other creditworthy borrower have made an agreement to add you as an authorized user to their lines of credit
They will never provide you with credit cards or account numbers that would permit you to make charges against the accounts
The combination of extended history and expanded credit limits will be merged into your credit history

Your benefit is an improvement in your personal credit rating. However, this does not change your entire credit record, it only increases your credit score as a result of the newly added tradeline.
